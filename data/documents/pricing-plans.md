# Fluffy Cake Pricing

Fluffy Cake offers three pricing tiers designed for teams of different sizes.

The Starter plan is free and includes one workspace, 10,000 embedding operations per month, 100 MB of vector storage, and community support. It is ideal for prototyping and small experiments.

The Team plan costs 99 euros per month and includes five workspaces, 500,000 embedding operations, 10 GB of vector storage, two model serving endpoints, and email support with a 24-hour response time.

The Enterprise plan offers custom pricing with unlimited workspaces, dedicated infrastructure, SLA guarantees, SSO integration, audit logs, and a dedicated customer success manager.
